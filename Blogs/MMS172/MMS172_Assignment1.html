For this project I'd like to do a short audiobook of "The Little Prince", specifically BAE Jin Soo's very short and nuanced version of the book which can be read in his webtoon "Friday: Forbidden Tales". After reading the webcomic I thought covering the whole thing in the audio book will be difficult since it is too long, and I'd like to focus on the alterations the author did to the story, so I might have to start the audiobook from the point where the little prince decides to leave his planet



Project Plan


Schedule

During the research period, I will be researching more about what goes into recording an audiobook or similar outputs. I aim to study during that week how to effectively tell a story with a vivid illustration of the environment or settings without the sound effects overpowering the story narration. I will also have to research how to manipulate audio clips to distort them and highlight the severity of the situation in the story. My aim here is to figure out a way to highlight the referenced incident in the snippet of the short story.

In determining and arranging samples, I will be listing the kind of sounds I will need to record or recreate for the audiobook while also arranging those sounds in the timeline of the audio story. Recording the length of the narration and the dialogues may help a lot to figure out the timing and arrangement of the sounds in the background.

It is pretty much self-explanatory in recording, mixing, & editing. I will be recording the needed material I listed from the previous period. Around the same time I will be mixing and editing the audio clips as well. I assigned the recording and the mixing to be around the same time because I predict I will be going back and forth from recording (or re-recording) and mixing audio clips.

Lastly, aside from the submission for the class requirement, I will upload the output here in my personal website. After some time has passed I will archive these blog posts and move the output somewhere else in the site (probably will create a new option in the navigation menu like "Projects"
